Ahyeon

On January 16, Ahyeon was introduced through a live performance as the second member of BABYMONSTER. On November 15, it was announced that Ahyeon recently left BABYMONSTER due to health reasons.However, it was later confirmed that Ahyeon will remain a member of the group and will not participate in debut activities due to health reasons. On January 25, 2024, YG announced that Ahyeon will be debuting with BABYMONSTER with their first mini album BABYMONS7ER on April 1.
